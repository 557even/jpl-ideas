```===========================================================
              JPL $300B Improvement Plan Summary
===========================================================

**Objective**: 
To enhance the performance, impact, and efficiency of JPL (Jet Propulsion Laboratory) by fostering a culture of continuous improvement, innovation, and sustainability in space exploration and scientific research.

**Key Investment Areas**:
- **Research & Development (R&D)**: Accelerate space exploration, astrobiology, and quantum research.
- **Technology Development**: Innovate in propulsion systems, AI, robotics, and autonomous spacecraft.
- **Operations Efficiency**: Streamline mission control, data systems, and resource management.
- **Workforce Development**: Enhance diversity, skills, and leadership within JPL's workforce.
- **Global Collaboration**: Strengthen international partnerships and collaborative missions.
- **Sustainability**: Focus on eco-friendly technologies, space debris management, and long-term human exploration.
